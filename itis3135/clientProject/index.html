A New World

Welcome to RenaissanceCraft, a challenging and immersive modded Minecraft survival experience built on Fabric 1.21.1. Designed for players seeking depth and danger, this server runs in hard mode and introduces survival mechanics like thirst and temperature management. You'll explore a richly expanded world featuring new and overhauled structures, biomes, and dungeons, all ripe for adventure and conquest.

Face these new perils with powerful tools from the past: authentic armor and weaponry from the Middle Ages and Renaissance. Travel across land and sea with immersive features like sailable ships and horse-drawn carts.

Build and Fight for Your Kingdom

Join or found a Faction using in-game commands to claim land that scales with your group size. Claimed chunks are protected—until war is declared. In wartime, the death of players compromises this protection, opening land to pillage and conquest. Forge alliances to create powerful coalitions, dominate your enemies, and secure your territory.

The Modpack

The server runs the RenaissanceCraft Modpack, featuring firearms, melee weapons, shields, armor, and ships from the Renaissance era. Enhanced with shaders, it delivers stunning visuals and immersive gameplay. You can download or learn more about the modpack here: RenaissanceCraft on Modrinth.
