FACTIONS

Mercenaries⚔️

The Mercenaries are a neutral, battle-hardened faction offering their swords and ships to the highest bidder. Operating as soldiers for hire, they can be recruited by any faction for war efforts, acting as a flexible force of conflict or defense. Their rugged stronghold is perched high on a mountainside in the Yellowstone biome, centered around coordinates 461 100 1390.

New Venice⛪

Inspired by the historic city of canals, New Venice is a floating city nestled in the Deep Ocean biome near spawn at coordinates -685 64 266. With its picturesque architecture and strategic maritime location, New Venice serves as a neutral hub for trade and diplomacy, aiming to connect factions through commerce rather than war.
